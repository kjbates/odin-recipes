Ann and Don's Pasta Sauce and Meatballs

This recipe is my Aunt and Uncle's pasta sauce and meatball recipe

This sauce is amazing if left to simmer and doesn't take a lot of effort. It can be either made on the stove or in a slow cooker.

Ingredients for Pasta Sauce:

  • 1 lg can tomato paste
  • 2 lg cans tomato sauce
  • 1 lg can tomatoes (crush)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp chili peppers
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp steak spice

Ingredients for Meatballs:

  • 1 ½ lbs. Lean ground beef
  • 2 cups finely ground cracker crumbs. Oatmeal also works.
  • ½ cup Kraft parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 garlic cloves chopped fine
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp steak spice

Directions for Pasta Sauce:

  1. In a good size pot, lightly fry tomato paste with about 1 tablespoon of oil for roughly one minute, making sure you constantly stir.
  2. Add all the rest of the ingredients. Stir well, bring to a slow boil (be careful not to overdue the heat, it will burn on the bottom and give the sauce a very poor taste).
  3. Add the meatballs and simmer for four hours.

Directions for Meatballs:

  1. mix all ingredients well, take a seat and roll into desired sized balls
  2. Place balls on cookie sheet and bake in oven at 350 degrees until lightly browned.
  3. Place meatballs in sauce and boil sauce slowly for 4 hours.
